Common Questions

Things clinicians ask us.

Do I have to speak in a clinical way?▼
No. Scribble is trained on real clinician language, not textbook jargon. Talk to your patients the way you normally do, and Scribble will map it to OASIS correctly.
Does it work without WiFi?▼
Yes. Scribble records locally on your phone. Once you're back in WiFi coverage, the visit syncs automatically for processing and EHR integration.
What if I don't trust an answer?▼
You review every field before submission. If Scribble's suggestion doesn't match what you said, edit it. You're always in control.
What do I tell patients?▼
You can tell them that you are using a note taker for charting. What helps you helps the patient.
How long does training take?▼
Most clinicians are comfortable with Scribble after 2–3 visits. One 45-minute onboarding session is all it takes to get started.
What if the visit is noisy, like TV on or dogs barking?▼
Scribble is trained on real home environments, not clinical settings. Background noise doesn't stop it from capturing what matters clinically. It filters for the conversation between you and your patient.
