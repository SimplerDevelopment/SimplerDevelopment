H3: 3+ hours of documentation daily
  P: Clinicians spend more time on indirect care than on actual patient visits, and most of it happens after hours, at home.
H3: Highest attrition rate in healthcare
  P: Home health loses experienced clinicians faster than any other care setting. Burnout from documentation is the leading driver.

  P: Recruitment, onboarding, and lost productivity add up fast. Keeping your clinicians happy is your highest-ROI investment.

H2: Real-time documentation. Less post-visit catch-up.
  P: Scribble listens during the visit, understands clinical context, and fills OASIS fields automatically, while you focus entirely on your patient.
H3: Activate at the door
  P: One tap before you enter. Scribble runs silently in the background, with no special phrasing or narration required. Just talk to your patient naturally.
H3: OASIS fills in real time
  P: As the visit unfolds, Scribble identifies clinically relevant responses and maps them to OASIS fields automatically. Every answer is traceable back to the conversation.
H3: Review and submit
  P: After the visit, review the completed documentation in under 5 minutes. Make any adjustments and push directly to your EHR, or your coding team.
